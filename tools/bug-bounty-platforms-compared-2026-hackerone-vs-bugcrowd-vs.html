❓ Choosing a bug bounty platform — 5 FAQs

1. Which platform pays the most?

HackerOne has paid over $300M, Bugcrowd $100M+, Immunefi $100M+ (DeFi). For open-source, huntr offers $1.5K–$4K MFV.

2. What platform is best for smart contracts?

Immunefi is #1 for DeFi/smart contracts. HackerOne and Bugcrowd also have solid blockchain programs.

3. Which platforms have the fastest response?

HackerOne and Bugcrowd average 24-48h. Immunefi varies, but some programs respond within hours. Intigriti and YesWeHack are also responsive.

4. Are there platforms with low fees?

huntr (open source) has 0% platform fee. Intigriti and YesWeHack charge ~15-20%. HackerOne/Bugcrowd vary by program.

5. Which platform for AI / mobile?

0din.ai focuses on AI/ML security. HackerOne and Bugcrowd have mobile & AI programs. YesWeHack covers web+mobile.
